Enriching Students Through Music

Welcome Educators and Administrators. Beyond event performance, Keys by Caleb is passionate about sharing the joy of music through engaging piano programs. Based in Riverside, CA, I aim to provide enriching musical experiences tailored to students in charter schools and other educational settings.

Potential Program Offerings

Flexible options to suit your school's needs (examples for discussion).

  • After-School Piano Club: Structured group sessions focusing on foundational skills, basic music reading/theory, ear training, and playing simple, fun songs. Typically weekly.
  • Introductory Piano Workshops: Short-term workshops (single or multi-session) designed to introduce students to the piano/keyboard in an interactive and engaging way.
  • Custom Curriculum Integration: Open to collaborating with schools to develop programs that align with specific educational goals, themes, or existing music programs.
  • Teaching Philosophy: Emphasis on a positive, encouraging learning environment, fostering creativity, and building a solid musical foundation through both traditional and intuitive methods (including playing by ear).

Logistical Considerations

Basic requirements for hosting lesson programs.

  • Space: A suitable room (classroom, music room, multi-purpose area) free from excessive noise and distractions is needed during lesson/workshop times.
  • Equipment: Programs are most effective if the school has access to pianos or digital keyboards. Options for providing portable keyboards can be discussed if needed.
  • Scheduling: Availability typically aligns with after-school hours, but specific workshop times can be arranged based on mutual availability. Let's discuss your school's calendar.
  • Group Size/Age: Programs can be adapted for various age groups (e.g., K-2, 3-5, Middle School) and reasonable class sizes. Details determined based on program type and space.

What Keys by Caleb Provides

My commitment to your students and school.

  • Experienced, patient, and engaging instruction tailored to different learning styles.
  • Adaptable lesson plans, potential curriculum materials, and supplementary resources.
  • Commitment to fostering student engagement, building confidence, and providing positive reinforcement.
  • Professionalism, reliability, and completion of all necessary background checks and clearances as required by your institution.

Benefits for Your School & Students

Bringing the power of music education to your community.

  • Offer a valuable and popular musical enrichment program to your students and families.
  • Help develop students' creativity, cognitive skills, discipline, fine motor coordination, and self-expression.
  • Provide a fun, accessible, and positive introduction to playing a musical instrument.
  • Enhance your school's reputation and offerings within the community.
