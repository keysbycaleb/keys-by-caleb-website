Package Scope: Just One Moment

This package includes up to one (1) continuous hour of live piano performance. It is designed for a single segment of your event, such as the wedding ceremony (including prelude and processional/recessional), a brief cocktail hour, background dinner music, or specific moments like first dances. It does not cover multiple separate parts of a larger event day. Performance time begins at the agreed-upon start time.

Payment & Confirmation

Payment for the Just One Moment package (**$495** + potential travel fees, confirmed via email) is processed securely via Stripe. Clicking 'Proceed to Secure Payment' will redirect you to Stripe to complete the transaction. Your booking is confirmed only upon successful payment and subsequent confirmation email from Caleb. Caleb will reach out within 24-48 hours after payment to finalize all details, including song selections and logistics. Standard cancellation policies apply (details provided upon confirmation).
